About Employee Attrition Prediction

Employee attrition can significantly impact businesses, leading to increased costs and lost productivity. Our prediction tool helps HR teams analyze patterns and make data-driven decisions to reduce turnover. By identifying at-risk employees early, organizations can implement proactive retention strategies, improve employee satisfaction, and ultimately reduce recruitment costs. This tool transforms complex machine learning outputs into accessible insights through an interactive interface designed for HR professionals—no technical expertise required.

How It Works

Our model analyzes key employee data—such as overtime, years in current role, job level, and more—to predict the likelihood of attrition. This enables HR teams to act early and implement targeted strategies to retain valuable talent.
