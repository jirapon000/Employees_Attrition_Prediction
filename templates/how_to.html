How to Use the Employee Attrition Prediction Tool

This tool helps HR professionals predict employee attrition by analyzing key factors. Simply enter the required details and let our AI do the rest.

Step 1: Employee Age

Provide the employee’s age to help determine experience level.

Step 2: Overtime Status

Indicate whether the employee works overtime (Yes/No).

Step 3: Job Role

Select the employee’s job role (e.g., Developer, Manager).

Step 4: Job Level

Enter the employee’s job level (1-5 based on seniority).

Step 5: Years in Current Role

Specify how long the employee has been in their current role.

Step 6: Job Involvement

Rate employee engagement in their role (1-5).

Step 7: Marital Status

Indicate whether the employee is single, married, or divorced.

Step 8: Job Satisfaction

Rate the employee’s job satisfaction (1-5).
